Bougainville Chocolate Festival 2025

28th to 30th October 2025 • Hutjena Oval, Buka

About the Festival

The Bougainville Chocolate Festival celebrates the region’s world-class cocoa, connecting local farmers, chocolatiers, and international buyers. Experience the taste of Bougainville’s finest cocoa and learn about sustainable cocoa farming and chocolate production.
